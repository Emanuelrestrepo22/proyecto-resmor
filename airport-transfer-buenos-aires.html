Coverage

Both airports, all of Buenos Aires.

Ministro Pistarini International Airport (EZE / Ezeiza) — the main international gateway, ~35 km from downtown. Transfer takes 45-60 minutes depending on traffic.

Aeroparque Jorge Newbery (AEP) — domestic flights and some regional, located right by the river in Buenos Aires city. Transfer is usually 15-25 minutes.

We drop you off anywhere in CABA (the autonomous city) and the metropolitan area (AMBA). Common destinations: Palermo, Recoleta, Microcentro, Belgrano, Puerto Madero, San Telmo.

FAQ

Common questions.

How much is an airport transfer from Ezeiza?

We quote each transfer individually based on destination, time of day, and number of passengers. Send us a WhatsApp and you'll get a flat-rate quote in minutes.

Does the driver speak English?

Yes. Our team supports English, Spanish, and Portuguese — clarify your preference when you book.

How early should I book?

We recommend booking at least 12 hours before your pickup time. For peak hours (Sunday evenings, holidays) book 24-48 hours ahead.

Do you wait if my flight is delayed?

Yes. We track your flight and waiting time is included in the flat rate.

How do I pay?

Cash (ARS or USD), bank transfer, or Mercado Pago. We confirm payment method when you book.
